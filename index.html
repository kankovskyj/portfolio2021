I start my design process with a discovery meeting. Then I do research, and turn the data I gathered into sketches/low fidelity wireframes in Invision .

After consultations, I use UsabilityHub for user testing and then proceed with high-fidelity wireframes in Figma or Adobe XD

Depending on the project, I either hand off the designs to developers, or start coding. Mostly using React and Sass.
